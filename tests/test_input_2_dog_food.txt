Service: "Happy Hound Meals" Fresh Food Subscription

What we do:
We deliver freshly cooked, vet-approved dog meals directly to your door every week. Unlike dry kibble sitting on supermarket shelves for months, Happy Hound Meals are slow-cooked in our kitchens using human-grade ingredients and flash-frozen to preserve nutrients.

Audience:
Dog owners who consider their pets to be family members and want to ensure they get the healthiest possible diet, but are too busy to cook for their dogs themselves.

Key Features & Benefits:
- 100% Real Food: Chicken, beef, carrots, spinach, and brown rice. No mystery meat, no artificial preservatives, and no fillers.
- Custom Portions: You take a quick online quiz about your dog's age, weight, and activity level. We send perfectly portioned meals.
- Convenient Delivery: Arrives every Sunday in a refrigerated, eco-friendly box. Just thaw, open, and serve.
- Vet Designed: Recipes formulated by animal nutritionists to support a shiny coat, healthy digestion, and long lifespan.

Pricing:
Starts at $25 per week depending on the size of the dog. Shipping is always free.

Main Value Proposition:
Give your best friend the long, healthy life they deserve, without spending hours meal prepping in the kitchen.
