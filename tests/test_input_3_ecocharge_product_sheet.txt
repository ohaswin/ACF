EcoCharge Power Bank

Introduction
The EcoCharge is our latest innovation in sustainable portable power. Engineered entirely from recycled ocean plastics and featuring a next-generation high-density solid-state battery, it offers true eco-friendly charging without sacrificing performance.

Target Audience
Eco-conscious travelers, remote workers, students, and outdoor enthusiasts who need reliable power on the go but want to minimize their carbon footprint.

Key Features & Specifications
*   Materials: 85% certified recycled ocean plastics.
*   Battery Capacity: 20,000mAh solid-state lithium. No risk of swelling or thermal runaway.
*   Charging Speed: 65W Power Delivery (PD) through USB-C. Can charge a standard 13-inch laptop to 50% in 45 minutes.
*   Ports: 2x USB-C (In/Out 65W, Out 20W), 1x USB-A (18W QuickCharge 3.0).
*   Solar Integration: Features a small auxiliary solar strip. (Note to marketing: specify this is a trickle charger for emergencies only, requiring 48 hours of direct sun to fully charge the unit).
*   Weight: 280 grams (incredibly light for its capacity).
*   Dimensions: 5.5" x 2.8" x 0.6".
*   Price: $99.99 MSRP.

Value Proposition
Power your journey while protecting the planet. The EcoCharge delivers enough juice to charge your smartphone five times over or power your laptop through a long flight, all packed into a sleek, ultra-light shell made from recovered ocean waste. It is the perfect marriage of high-tech performance and environmental responsibility.

Warranty Information
We offer a 5-year replacement warranty, double the industry standard, ensuring you keep using the EcoCharge for years instead of adding to e-waste.
