Product: "BrewMaster Connect" Smart Coffee Maker

Overview:
The BrewMaster Connect is the ultimate morning companion for coffee lovers. It's a premium drip coffee maker that pairs directly with your smartphone, so you can start a fresh pot of coffee without getting out of bed. 

Key Features:
- Mobile App Control: Start, stop, and schedule brewing from your phone via Bluetooth and Wi-Fi.
- Built-in Grinder: Grinds whole beans just seconds before brewing for maximum freshness.
- Auto-Schedule: Set a 7-day schedule so your coffee is always ready right when your alarm goes off.
- Keep Warm Mode: An insulated carafe keeps coffee hot for up to 4 hours without burning it.

Target Audience:
Busy professionals, parents, and anyone who struggles to wake up in the morning and wants the convenience of coffee waiting for them.

Value Proposition:
Wake up to the smell of fresh coffee every single day. The BrewMaster Connect saves you 10 minutes every morning and guarantees cafe-quality coffee at the touch of a single button on your phone.

Price: $149.99

Notes for the copywriter:
- The app requires a Wi-Fi connection for the scheduling feature to work. (Let's make sure we flag this so people aren't confused if their Wi-Fi goes down).
- Let's focus on the feeling of a stress-free morning.
